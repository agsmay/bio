About Me

I'm a penultimate year Aerospace Engineering and Mathematics student at the University of Sydney, with a passion for embedded systems, control systems and their application in space systems. I have recently completed a project in a team of 8 developing a prototype CubeSat, "SELENE", which we have been selected to present at the 11th UNISEC Global Meeting in Tokyo, Japan.

I am a casual academic at the University of Sydney, teaching, developing and revising course materials for core second year units. As a research intern I was involved in developing new software for the school of Aerospace, Mechanical and Mechatronics' state of the art flight simulators. As a lead for USYD Rocketry, I have exceptional interpersonal skills such as communication and collaboration.
